Main Headline Article: Major trade updates

Trade Alert: DT Dexter Lawrence has been traded to the Cincinnati Bengals. The Bengals give the New York Giants 2026 First round, Pick 10 in exchange for Star Defensive Linemen, Dexter Lawrence. Lawrence was given a contract extention of a one-year, $28 million extension just short after being traded.

Featured: Trade Impact & Fan Poll

What This Trade Means

The Bengals acquiring Dexter Lawrence strengthens their defensive front significantly, giving them a dominant interior presence. This move signals a “win-now” mentality, especially as they aim to compete deep into the playoffs.

For the Giants, gaining a 2026 first-round pick opens the door for rebuilding and adding young talent. While losing a star defensive lineman hurts, the long-term flexibility could reshape the franchise’s future.
